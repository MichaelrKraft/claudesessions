Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free version really free forever?

Yes. The core features (auto-archive, search, checkpoints, web UI) will always be free and open source under the MIT license.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free is 100% local-only. Pro adds encrypted cloud backup, multi-device sync, and mobile access. Team Pro adds shared workspaces and collaboration features.

Can I use Claude Sessions commercially on the free plan?

Yes! The MIT license allows commercial use. Pro is optional for teams who need collaboration features.

When will Pro be available?

We're launching Pro Q1 2025. Sign up for early access at the GitHub repo.

Do you offer discounts for students or open source projects?

Yes! Contact us at hello@claudesessions.com with proof of student status or open source work.

What payment methods do you accept?

We'll accept all major credit cards and PayPal when Pro launches. Annual plans will get 2 months free.
